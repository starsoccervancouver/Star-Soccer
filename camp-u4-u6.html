Summer camp

Mini Stars Camp

U4-U6

This camp is for younger players who are just beginning their football journey and need a fun, welcoming summer environment.

All sessions run on the Kitsilano High School turf, weekday mornings from 9 AM to 12 PM, through the final week of August.

Camp info

Summer payment options

Mini Stars sessions run from 9 AM to 12 PM. Families can pay online for the full week or pay $55 cash for a drop-in session.
