This summer program is built for younger players who are just starting their football journey and need a welcoming introduction to structure, movement, and the ball.

Sessions are mixed boys and girls and focus on enjoyment, simple habits, and building confidence in a positive environment.

Program philosophy

Mini Stars Approach

Who it is for

Young players who are learning the basics of movement, confidence, teamwork, and how to enjoy a structured football session.

How it feels

Welcoming, active, and encouraging, with simple coaching language and plenty of repetition through play.

Next step

This group prepares players for Soccer Stars by building comfort on the ball and stronger group-training habits.

Mini Stars gives younger players the right first experience: fun, organized, and full of confidence-building touches.
