Star Soccer identity directions

Minimal concepts based on your current brand.

The current graphic has recognizable ingredients: a star, motion, soccer, and dark contrast. To minimalize it, I kept those ingredients but stripped out the clutter, extra effects, and dated shapes.

Refined Original

Minimalized Current Logo

This keeps the actual structure of your current mark: outlined star, purple motion shape, ball overlap, and stacked red wordmark. It is just cleaner, sharper, and easier to use on a website.

Concept 1

Star + Swoosh

Closest to your current identity. It keeps the movement arc from the old logo, but removes the ball clip-art and makes the mark feel cleaner and more athletic.

Concept 2

Monoline Badge

More premium and organized. This would work well if you want the site to feel credible for parents while still looking modern on camp shirts and social graphics.

Concept 3

Minimal Wordmark

The simplest direction. Strong for a clean website header and easiest to scale into a more modern brand system.

If the goal is "same logo, just better," use the Refined Original first. It preserves the identity you already have without bringing all of the old visual clutter forward.
