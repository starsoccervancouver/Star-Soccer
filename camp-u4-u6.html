Star Soccer Vancouver

This camp is for younger players who are just beginning their football journey and need a fun, welcoming summer environment.

Sessions run weekday mornings (9:30 AM–12 PM) and afternoons (12:30–3 PM) at Kitsilano High School Turf and Trimble Park. Trimble Park sessions are run by head coach Evan.
