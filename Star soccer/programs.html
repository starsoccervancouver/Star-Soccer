Programs

Star Soccer Vancouver

Star Soccer programs are year-round and run by season, so families can stay in the same overall pathway while the focus shifts through fall, winter, spring, and summer.

The upcoming season is Summer, and all program groups will be mixed boys and girls with age-appropriate coaching, clear expectations, and a consistent Star Soccer training standard.
