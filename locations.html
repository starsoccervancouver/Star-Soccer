Star Soccer Vancouver

Where we train

Kits Turf & Trimble Park

Star Soccer camps and programs run at two excellent Vancouver venues — Kitsilano High School Turf and Trimble Park. Both are well-maintained, easy to reach by car or transit, and have clear parent viewing areas.

Main camp venue

Kitsilano High School Turf

2295 W 10th Ave, Vancouver, BC V6K 2H9

Our primary summer camp venue. A full-size artificial turf field right at Kitsilano High School with plenty of space for all age groups to train simultaneously. Easy street parking along W 10th Ave and W Broadway, with a clear parent viewing area along the sideline.

Mini Stars Camp (U4–U6) Soccer Stars Camp (U7–U10) Soccer Stars Advanced (U11–U14) Weekly programs

Second camp venue

Trimble Park

NW Marine Dr & Trimble St, Vancouver, BC

Our Point Grey camp site at Trimble Park sits right off NW Marine Drive with a great grass surface and a relaxed, open setting. Coach Evan leads morning and afternoon sessions here across all three camp levels throughout the summer. Street parking available on Trimble St and nearby residential streets.

Mini Stars Camp (U4–U6) Soccer Stars Camp (U7–U10) Soccer Stars Advanced (U11–U14)
