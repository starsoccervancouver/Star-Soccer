Summer camp

Soccer Stars Camp

U11-U14

This camp is for older players who want a sharper football standard, faster decision-making, and a more demanding training environment.

Sessions run weekday mornings (9:30 AM–12 PM) and afternoons (12:30–3 PM) at Kitsilano High School Turf and Trimble Park. Trimble Park sessions are run by head coach Evan.

Camp info

Summer payment options

Sessions run 9:30 AM–12 PM and 12:30–3 PM at Kitsilano High School Turf and Trimble Park (Coach Evan). Register online for the full week or pay $55 cash for a drop-in.
